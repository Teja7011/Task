Fun Ways to Format Your Text

When you start building websites, you'll want to play around with how your words look. You can make text bold just for looks, or use strong to show it's actually important. If you want a slight slant, you can make things italic for style or use emphasis to change how a sentence sounds. Need to remember something? You can highlight it like a marker, or drop the size down for fine print and legal footnotes. If you're editing a draft, you can show words you deleted with a line through them and new words with an underline. Lastly, you can use superscripts for math formulas like E=mc² and subscripts for science specs like H₂O.
